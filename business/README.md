# business/

Already have a company? Put everything you have in here: contracts, invoices you made before, your logo, letterhead, your registration extract. Any format, any mess; drag it in.

This folder stays in YOUR private copy of the castle, never in the public one.

During setup Claude asks you one question about this folder: organize it together first, or keep it as it is. Either way, Claude fills your company profile (`design/company.yaml`) and colors from what it finds here, so invoices and letters come out in your house style. It never guesses official numbers: only values literally found in your documents are used, and anything not found stays empty until you provide it.
